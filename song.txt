Parno sin, muri romnyi parno sin,
De kamav la,kemera andala!
Lasho sin,muri romnyi lashi sin,
Maj azért de nagyon kamavla,
Shukar-shukar cirna romnya.

He cino ri bomba,He cino ri bomba-bomba,
He cino ori bomba,kama fura romnya,Devla.
Acalari bomba,acalari bomba-bomba,
Acalari bomba,side laya cirni bomba!

Devla cirtaba,ketyila misa va,
De romenge,tajburan romnyelel!
Lasho sin,muri romyni lashi sin,
Maj azért de nagyon kamavla,
Shukar-shukar cirna romnya.

Acalari bomba,acalari bomba-bomba,
Acalari bomba,side lata rari bomba!
Acalari bomba,acalari bomba-bomba,
Acalari bomba,side laya cirni bomba!
